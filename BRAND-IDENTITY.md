# AnouarLab Brand Identity

---

## Brand Name
**AnouarLab**

---

## Tagline
*"SEO et CRO data-driven pour Shopify et WordPress"*

---

## Brand Mission
Helping e-commerce businesses grow through **data-driven optimization, innovative experimentation, and measurable results**, specializing in SEO and CRO for Shopify and WordPress stores.

---

## Brand Values
1. **Innovation** – Leveraging modern tools and data to stay ahead.  
2. **Growth** – Driving measurable business results for clients.  
3. **Trust** – Transparent, ethical, and reliable consulting.  
4. **Experimentation** – Testing, analyzing, and refining strategies.  
5. **Professionalism** – Delivering high-quality, premium services.  

---

## Logo
- **Style:** Modern, minimalist, geometric, tech-inspired, scalable.  
- **Iconography:** Abstract, growth/data/motion-inspired (bars, arrows, dots).  
- **Typography:** Bold geometric sans-serif, readable, premium.  
- **Deliverables:** Vector logo (for web, social media, print).  
- **Backgrounds:** Transparent, light, and dark versions.  

---

## Color Palette

| Role                     | Color                  | HEX      | RGB          |
|--------------------------|-----------------------|----------|--------------|
| Primary                  | Dark Olive Green      | #32452A  | 50, 69, 42   |
| Accent                   | Lime Green            | #9ACD32  | 154, 205, 50 |
| Secondary / Highlight    | Soft Mint / Muted Cream| #EEEED1 | 238, 238, 209|
| Optional Highlight       | Muted Greenish Cream  | #CBEBC0  | 203, 227, 192|

**Usage Guidelines:**  
- Dark Olive Green → text, main icon shapes.  
- Lime Green → accent, highlights, growth arrows, call-to-action elements.  
- Soft Mint / Cream → secondary icon shapes, subtle backgrounds, infographics.  

---

## Typography
- **Primary Font:** Modern geometric sans-serif (bold and readable). Examples: Inter, Poppins, Montserrat.  
- **Use Cases:**  
  - **Logo & Headings:** Bold, all caps optional.  
  - **Body Text:** Regular weight, clean, readable.  

---

## Iconography / Graphic Style
- Abstract, minimalist shapes representing:  
  - Growth (ascending bars, arrows)  
  - Data / Analytics (dots, network nodes)  
  - Motion / Progress (curves, trajectories)  
- Refined, balanced, premium feel, no clutter.  

---

## Brand Voice / Tone
- **Professional, confident, and growth-oriented.**  
- **Friendly, approachable, but authoritative** in advice.  
- Focused on **results, experimentation, and data-driven decisions**.  

---

## Applications
1. **Website:** Logo, icons, CTA buttons in accent color, clean layout.  
2. **Social Media:** Profile/logo adaptations, neon yellow for highlights.  
3. **Print / Business Cards:** Minimalist with dark olive green background, neon yellow accents.  
4. **Presentations:** Charts in brand colors, bold headings, modern geometric icons.
